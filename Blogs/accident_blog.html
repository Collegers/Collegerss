The Accident

The Community's Response

As news of the accident spread, the entire Free Fire community rallied together, sending thoughts, prayers, and well-wishes for Gyan Sujan’s recovery. Known for his resilience and positivity, Gyan Sujan's spirit has been a beacon of inspiration for many. On April 29, there was a glimmer of hope as he was moved from the ICU to a regular cabin, showing signs of recovery.
